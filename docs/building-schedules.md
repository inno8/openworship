# Building Schedules

This guide explains how to create a schedule, add songs and other items, reorder them, and use custom text and blank slides.

---

## What is a schedule?

A **schedule** is a single setlist for a service. It contains **items** in order:

- **Songs** — From your Library; each shows its verses/slides in Presenter.
- **Custom text** — Your own text (e.g. scripture, announcements, welcome).
- **Blank slides** — Black (or blank) slides for moments when you don’t want lyrics on screen.

You can have multiple schedules (e.g. “Sunday AM”, “Sunday PM”, “Wednesday”) and switch between them. The **Presenter** uses the **active** schedule when you’re on the Schedule tab.

---

## Creating or opening a schedule

1. Open **Schedule** from the sidebar.
2. If no schedule exists, the app creates one (e.g. “Sunday Service”) and sets it as active.
3. To **rename** the current schedule: click the pencil icon next to the title, type the new name, then click away or press Enter.
4. The **date** under the title helps you tell schedules apart; you can have one schedule per service date.

To add more schedules (if your app supports multiple): create or switch via the same Schedule view or any schedule selector your church uses. The **active** schedule is the one used in Presenter.

---

## Adding songs to the schedule

1. In **Schedule**, the right side shows **“Add Songs”** with a search box and a grid of songs.
2. Use the **search** box to find songs by title or author.
3. Click the **+** button on a song card to add that song to the **end** of the current schedule.

The new item appears in the list on the left. You can reorder it (see below).

---

## Reordering items

On the left, each schedule item has:

- **Up / Down arrows** — Move the item one position up or down.
- Items at the top/bottom can’t move further in that direction.

Reorder until the setlist matches the service order. The order in the list is the order you’ll see in **Presenter**.

---

## Adding custom text items

Use this for scripture, announcements, welcome text, or any text that isn’t a song.

1. In **Schedule**, click the **custom text** button (document icon) next to **+ Add Item**.
2. In the dialog:
   - **Title** — Short label (e.g. “Scripture Reading”, “Announcements”). Shown in the schedule list and in Presenter.
   - **Text Content** — The exact text to show on screen (e.g. “John 3:16 — For God so loved the world…”). Line breaks are kept.
3. Click **Add to Schedule** (or **Update** if editing).

The item is added to the end of the schedule. In **Presenter**, selecting it shows the text as one or more slides (split by lines as needed). You can reorder or edit it like any other item.

### Editing custom text

1. In the schedule list, find the custom text item.
2. Click the **pencil (edit)** icon on that row.
3. Change the title or text content, then click **Update**.

---

## Adding blank slides

Use a **blank** item when you want a black (or empty) screen (e.g. before the offering, during prayer).

1. In **Schedule**, click the **blank slide** button (rectangle icon) next to **+ Add Item**.
2. A “Blank Slide” item is added at the end of the schedule.

In **Presenter**, when you select that item and go live, the output shows a black slide. You can also use the **Black** button during the service for an instant blackout without changing the selected item.

---

## Saving and loading schedules

- Schedules are **saved automatically** as you add, remove, or reorder items and when you rename the schedule. You don’t need to click “Save” separately.
- **Loading** means choosing which schedule is active:
  - In **Schedule**, the schedule you see and edit is the active one. If your setup has a schedule dropdown or list, select the one you want (e.g. “Sunday AM”).
  - In **Presenter**, the **Schedule** tab shows the **active** schedule’s items. Switch the active schedule in Schedule view first if you want to run a different setlist.

Data is stored locally (and optionally synced if you use the backend). Export backups from **Settings → Data Management → Export Backup** (see [Getting Started](getting-started.md)).

---

## Summary

| Task | How |
|------|-----|
| Create/rename schedule | Schedule opens with a default; click pencil next to title to rename. |
| Add song | Use “Add Songs” on the right; search and click **+** on a song. |
| Add custom text | Click the document icon → enter Title and Text Content → Add to Schedule. |
| Add blank slide | Click the rectangle (blank) icon. |
| Reorder | Use the up/down arrows on each item. |
| Edit custom text | Click the pencil on the custom item row → edit → Update. |
| Remove item | Click the X (remove) on the item row. |

Once the schedule is set, go to **Presenter**, select the schedule tab, and use **GO LIVE** and the verse list to run the service (see [Running a Service](running-a-service.md)).
